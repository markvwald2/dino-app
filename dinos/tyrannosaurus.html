King of the Dinosaurs

Tyrannosaurus was one of the largest meat-eating dinosaurs that ever lived. Everything about this ferocious predator, from its thick, heavy skull to its 4-foot-long jaw was designed for maximum bone-crushing action. Until recently, Tyrannosaurus rex was the biggest known carnivorous dinosaur; Giganotosaurus and Carcharodontosaurus have been found to be slightly bigger.

Say My Name:

tih-RAN-oh-SORE-us

Bite Me

Tyrannosaurus might have had the strongest bite of any animal that has ever lived: Three times the force of a great white shark, 15 times the force of an African lion, and 3 1/2 times the force of an Australian saltwater crocodile.



I See You

T. rex had strong depth perception, with eyes the size of a softballs. His vision would have been sharp -- maybe as sharp as its sense of smell.
