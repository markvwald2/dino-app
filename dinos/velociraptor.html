Small but Powerful

Velociraptors were vicious all right, but they were also very small--about the size of a toddler--and were likely covered in feathers. Although its sharp teeth were certainly unpleasant, the primary weapons in Velociraptor's arsenal were the curved, three-inch-long claws on its hind feet, which it used to slash and jab at prey.

A Fight for the Ages

One famous fossil shows a Velociraptor and Protoceratops locked in combat as they were both buried alive by a sudden sandstorm.



Desert Dino

The bones of this dinosaur were discovered in the remote Gobi Desert on the outskirts of Mongolia in 1922.
