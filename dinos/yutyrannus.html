Feathered Friend

Yutyrannus was giant meat-eating dinosaur which was recently discovered in China. It's one of the largest feathered tyrannosaurs ever and it has led scientists to wonder if large tyrannosaurs like T. Rex had feathers. Maybe they were green and scaly like reptiles or maybe they were soft and downy, like a giant baby duck.

Say My Name:

you-tih-RAN-us

China Dino

Three Yutyrannus fossils have been found in the rock beds in northeastern China. These are the largest known dinosaur specimens that preserve direct evidence of feathers.



T Rex Predecessor

Yutyrannus was the largest feathered animal that ever lived, and was an early version of Tyrannosaurus rex, clad in long, fuzzy filaments.
