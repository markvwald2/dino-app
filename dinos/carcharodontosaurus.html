One Mean Carnivore

Carcharodontosaurus was a huge meat eater with a massive tail, a bulky body, and heavy bones. Its arms were short and it had three-fingered hands with sharp claws. Although Carcharodontosaurus was larger than T. rex, its brain was smaller and it was a more primitive dinosaur than T. rex.

Say My Name:

kahr-KAR-o-DON-to-SAWR-us

Smarty Pants

Carcharodontosaurus was probably smarter than most dinosaurs, based on the size of its brain compared to the size of the rest of its body.



Fragile Fossils

The first known Carcharodontosaurus fossil was a skull and some bones found in North Africa in 1927. A larger one was found in North Africa in 1996.
