It Ran Like a Bird

Gallimimus was a fast-moving, bird-like dinosaur with a toothless beak. The bottom front part of its beak was shaped like a shovel. It had long legs, a long neck, a long tail, and hollow bones. This dinosaur had short arms with three clawed fingers on each hand, and long legs with three clawed toes on each.

Say My Name:

GAL-i-MIME-us

Dino, Dino, Goose?

A skull found in southern Mongolia reveals that Gallimimus' snout was flattened at the end like that of a modern-day goose.



Varied Diet

Gallimimus was a omnivore, but it didn't eat other dinosaurs. It at insects, lizards, small mammals, eggs, seeds and maybe carrion.
