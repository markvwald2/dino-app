Bird-like Killer

Troodon was a human-sized, fast, long-legged bipedal meat eater with serrated teeth and long, slim jaws. It may have been the smartest dinosaur, having the largest brain in proportion to its body weight (as smart as a modern bird). It had long hind legs, a stiff tail, large eyes and may have had a good sense of hearing.

Say My Name:

TRUE-oh-don

Claws-A-Plenty

Troodon had a large, sickle-shaped toe claw on its second toe. It also had three long, clawed fingers that were ideal for grasping prey.



Varied Diet

Troodon teeth are serrated - a feature often seen in animals eating a significant amount of plant food, so Troodon may have been omnivorous.
