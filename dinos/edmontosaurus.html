Cheeky Tooth

Edmontosaurus was a large, plant-eating, duck-billed dinosaur who had short arms, a long, pointed tail, three-toed, hoofed feet, and mitten-like hands. Its head was flat and sloping with a wide, toothless beak, cheek pouches, and hundreds of closely-packed cheek teeth that ground up its food.

Say My Name:

ed-MON-to-SAWR-us

Switch and Go Dino

Edmontosaurus' hind limbs were longer than its front limbs, which allowed it to move about on either two legs as well as four.



Nose Balloon?

Edmontosaurus had leathery skin and may have had loose skin around its nose area which some paleontologists think it was able to blow up like a balloon.
