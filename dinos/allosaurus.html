Mini T-Rex

A bit like a nimbler, scaled-down version of the much later Tyrannosaurus Rex, Allosaurus was one of the most common predatory dinosaurs of the Jurassic period. It was a fearsome meat-eater with sharp teeth and a well-muscled body, a prominent head, and distinctive ridges over its eyes.

Say My Name:

al-oh-SORE-us

Stegosaurus Enemy

Paleontologists found an Allosaurus fossil with a puncture wound that matches a Stegosaurus tail spike and a Stegosaurus neck bone bearing an Allosaurus-shaped bite mark.



Adult-size Teenager

Allosaurus may have reached its full adult size by age 15 and was likely capable of living another 10 or 15 years.
