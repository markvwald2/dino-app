Plates and Spikes

Stegosaurus had 17 bony plates that ran along its back and tail in two rows which alternated in alignment. These plates may have been used for protection or mating display purposes. Stegosaurus also had spikes at the end of its flexible tail, which were up to four feet long and were used for protection from predators.

Say My Name:

STEG-oh-SORE-us

Not a Brainiac

Stegosaurus had a brain the size of a walnut, which is about the size of a modern dog's. This is unusually small for an animal of Stegosaurus's size.



Eating Rocks

Like many herbivorous dinosaurs, Stegosaurus swallowed small rocks that helped mash up the tough vegetable matter in its enormous stomach.
